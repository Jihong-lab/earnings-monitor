NVIDIA Q3 Fiscal 2026 Financial Results Press Release
Date: November 19, 2025

FINANCIAL HIGHLIGHTS

Record Q3 FY2026 Results:
- Total revenue: $57.0 billion (up 22% QoQ, up 62% YoY)
- Data Center revenue: $51.2 billion (up 25% QoQ, up 66% YoY)
- Gaming revenue: $4.3 billion (down 1% QoQ, up 30% YoY)
- Professional Visualization revenue: $760 million (up 26% QoQ, up 56% YoY)
- Automotive revenue: $592 million (up 1% QoQ, up 32% YoY)

Profitability Metrics:
- GAAP gross margin: 73.4% (vs. 72.4% prior quarter, 74.6% year-ago)
- Non-GAAP gross margin: 73.6%
- GAAP diluted EPS: $1.30
- Non-GAAP diluted EPS: $1.30
- GAAP net income: $31.91 billion
- Operating income: $36.01 billion (GAAP), $37.75 billion (non-GAAP)

CEO COMMENTARY

Jensen Huang, founder and CEO, stated: "Blackwell sales are off the charts, and cloud GPUs are sold out. Compute demand keeps accelerating and compounding across training and inference — each growing exponentially."

- Non-GAAP gross margin: 75.0% (±50 basis points)
- GAAP operating expenses: ~$6.7 billion

KEY BUSINESS HIGHLIGHTS

Data Center Dominance:
NVIDIA revealed Blackwell achieved highest performance in SemiAnalysis InferenceMAX benchmarks with 10x throughput per megawatt versus prior generation. Strategic partnerships announced include:
- OpenAI: deploying at least 10 gigawatts of NVIDIA systems
- Anthropic: 1 gigawatt of compute capacity
- US Department of Energy: Solstice supercomputer with 100,000 Blackwell GPUs

Product Innovations:
- NVIDIA Rubin CPX unveiled for massive-context processing
- NVQLink introduced for GPU-quantum processor coupling
- NVIDIA BlueField-4 processor launched
- Omniverse DSX announced for AI factory design

Gaming and Visualization:
New titles launched with DLSS 4 Multi Frame Generation technology. GeForce celebrated 25 years with Seoul festival featuring RTX announcements.

Autonomous Systems:
NVIDIA DRIVE AGX Hyperion 10 platform introduced. Partnership with Uber announced targeting 100,000 level 4-ready vehicles by 2027.

Next quarterly dividend: $0.01 per share on December 26, 2025.
